The Fortifications of the Knights of St. John

Home Harbours Coastal Towers About Us

Defensive power was almost inexistent at the time of arrival of the Knights. The Order saw defence and protection of utmost importance due to other powers becoming interested in Malta. Firstly, they saw to the protection of the harbours by building numerous forts and fortifying Fort St Angelo. Later, it came to their knowledge the need of fortifying the naked coastline from which pirates could easily enter and embark. In the 17th century the Grandmasters Wignacourt, Lascaris and De Redin self-financed the building of several coastal watchtowers.
